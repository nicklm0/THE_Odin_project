Deep Dish Brownies

These brownies are the best you'll ever have. They are rich, moist, and full of chocolate. A true chocolate lover's dream.

prep time

60 mins

Servings

9

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cups of butter, melted
  • 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 1/2 cups of vanilla extract
  • 3egs
  • 3/4 cups of all all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cups of unsweetened coca powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt

Directions

  1. Oven to 350 deggrees F (175 degrees C) and grease am 8 inch square pan
  2. In a large bowl, blend melted butter, sugar and vanilla
  3. Beat in eggs one at the time.
  4. Conbine the four, cocoa baking powder and salt. Gradually blend into egg mixture
  5. Spread the batter into the prepared pan
  6. Bake for 40 to 45 minutes in the preheated oven, until brownies begin to pull away from the sides of the pan
  7. Let brownies cool, then cut into squares Enjoy

Tip

Aluminum foil can be used to keep the brownies moist, cook it evenly, and cleen up easier.
